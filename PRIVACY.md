# Privacy Policy - Resonance Retuner

**Effective date: 18 July 2026**

Resonance Retuner does not collect, transmit, sell or share personal information.

The extension stores only these settings in the user's local Chrome extension storage: enabled state, source reference, target reference, selected preset, automatic-source preference, latest estimated tuning and confidence score.

To provide its features, the extension accesses the active YouTube or YouTube Music page, locates the HTML5 media element, adjusts local playback properties and-only when the user presses Analyze-temporarily samples the media element's audio stream in memory. Audio samples are processed locally, are not recorded to disk, are not transmitted and are discarded when analysis finishes.

The extension does not read or collect account information, comments, search history, browsing history, form data, passwords, payment information or precise location. It contains no analytics, advertising or tracking SDK.

Preset descriptions are informational descriptions of cultural or sound-wellness associations. The extension does not provide medical advice and does not diagnose, treat, cure or prevent any condition.
