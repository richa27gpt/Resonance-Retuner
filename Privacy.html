Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

Overview

Resonance Retuner respects your privacy. The extension does not collect, sell, rent or share your personal information. All audio processing takes place locally inside your browser.

Information Stored

The extension stores only your preferences using Chrome's local extension storage.

  • Enabled / Disabled state
  • Selected source tuning
  • Selected target tuning
  • Selected preset
  • Latest locally calculated tuning estimate
  • User preferences

These settings remain on your own device and are never transmitted to us.

Audio Processing

When you press Analyze, the extension temporarily analyses the currently playing audio in your browser to estimate its musical reference tuning.

  • No audio is recorded.
  • No audio is uploaded.
  • No audio leaves your computer.
  • No cloud processing is performed.

Permissions

The extension requests only the permissions required for its functionality.

  • Storage — saves your tuning preferences locally.
  • Active Tab — communicates with the current supported YouTube tab.
  • YouTube Host Access — accesses supported YouTube pages in order to control playback and analyse audio locally when requested by the user.

Third-Party Services

Resonance Retuner does not use analytics services, advertising networks, tracking pixels or third-party data collection services.

Medical Disclaimer

Resonance Retuner allows users to explore alternative musical reference tunings. Descriptions of tuning traditions and sound-wellness concepts are provided for educational and informational purposes only. The extension does not diagnose, treat, cure or prevent any disease or medical condition and should not be considered medical advice.
